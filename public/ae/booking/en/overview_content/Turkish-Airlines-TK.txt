<p>Turkish Airlines is the flag carrier of Turkey and operates from its hub at the Ataturk Airport in Istanbul. The 4-Star airline is considered to be one of the best carriers as it has a global coverage as well as an impressive record of flight safety, reliability, and service quality. The airline is keen on enhancing its worldwide service levels. It also aims to provide you with fares that are at par with several low-cost carriers. Turkish Airlines is the 4th largest in the world in terms of footprint. It has been a member of the Star Alliance since 2008.</p><h2>Onboard Turkish Airlines</h2><p>Turkish Airways first began in 1933 as the "State Airlines Administration". By 1947, the national carrier of Turkey went international with its flight to Athens, Greece from Ankara and Istanbul in Turkey. The airline adopted its current name in 1955, and by 1973, it had catered to 2.5 million passengers. Turkish Airlines has become a global carrier in the true sense as it flies to virtually all the continents.</p><p>Turkish Airlines has won several accolades and awards over the years. It won Europe's Leading Airline for Business Class in 2010. The airline was also recognized as the Best Airline in Europe at the Skytrax World Airline Awards, 2016.
