<p>Ahmedabad, also called Amdavad, is a major city in the state of Gujarat. The major industry here is the cotton-milling industry. Others include vegetable oil, soap, glass, pharmaceuticals, and computer software, among others. Places to visit here include the Calico Museum, the Shreyas Folk Museum, and the Utensils Museum. The Sabarmati Ashram, the religious retreat of Mahatma Gandhi, the father of the nation, is also located here.</p><p><a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/india/ahmedabad/" target="_blank">Ahmedabad</a> is connected to other cities via its world-class airport known as the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/ahmedabad-airport.html" target="_blank">Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport</a>. The airport’s IATA code is AMD. It is well connected to the city via buses, taxis, and car rentals available on a regular basis. The airport also has facilities like banks, mobile phone recharging points, shops, retail outlets, luggage trolleys, spa facility, and a childcare room among others.</p><p>Some of the cities that fly here include Delhi, Chennai, Abu Dhabi, Mumbai, Dubai, Doha, Bengaluru, Jaipur, Pune, Hyderabad, and more.
