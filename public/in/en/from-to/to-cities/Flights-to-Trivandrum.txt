<p>Thiruvananthapuram, formerly known as Trivandrum, is the capital of the state of Kerala. It is located along the Arabian Sea and is known for its immense beauty. The major industries here include sugar milling, mineral processing, handicrafts, and textiles.. Places of interest here include the Museum of History and Heritage, Puthe Maliga Palace Museum, Natural History Museum, Kanakunna Palace, Shri Chitra Art Gallery, Napier Museum, and a lot more.</p><p><a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/india/thiruvananthapuram/" target="_blank">Thiruvananthapuram</a> is connected to the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/trivandrum-airport.html" target="_blank">Trivandrum International Airport</a>. The airport’s IATA code is TRV. It has two terminals and offers facilities such as cyber café, first aid, child care room, wheelchair services foreign exchange, car rental services, prepaid taxi services, and a whole lot more for its passengers’ convenience.</p><p>The cities that fly here include Delhi, Kuwait, Sharjah, Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Dammam, Chennai, Muscat, Kochi, Hyderabad, and others.
