<p>Pune, also spelled as Poona, is known as the “Queen of the Deccan.” This city is the cultural capital of the Marathas. The things to see here include the Sinhgad Fort, Bhimashankar, the Theravada Buddhist Karle and Bhaja caves, Dehu (the birthplace of the Marathi poet and saint Tukaram, and others. </p><p><a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/india/pune/" target="_blank">Pune</a> is currently connected to other cities via the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/pune-airport.html" target="_blank">Pune Airport</a> which is in Lohegaon, about 10 km away from the city. The airport’s IATA code is PNQ. It has only one terminal for both domestic as well as international flights. It has facilities like a medical room, childcare room, restaurants, post office, retiring rooms, baggage wrapping services, snack bars, spa services, radio cab service, music store, and prepaid and general taxi services, among others. At present, a new international airport is being built at Purandar to serve the city. It is expected to get ready by the year 2021.</p><p>The major cities connected to Pune are Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Frankfurt, Mumbai, Nagpur, Bangalore, Chennai, Kochi, Kolkata, and Indore, among others.
