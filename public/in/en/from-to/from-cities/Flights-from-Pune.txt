<p>Pune, also known as Poona, is called the “Queen of the Deccan.” This city is the cultural capital of the Marathas and has something for everyone. With its pleasant climate, <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/india/pune/" target="_blank">Pune</a> offers a refreshing holiday. Tourists also come here to view the museums, parks, historic artifacts, religious monuments, and other cultural attractions. As an educational center, the city has achieved a lot. It has around 30 affiliated and constituent colleges of the University of Pune. The Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute has more than 20,000 ancient manuscripts in Sanskrit and Pali. This is a renowned institute in the teaching and research of these articles.</p><p>The nearest airport to Pune is the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/pune-airport.html" target="_blank">Pune Airport</a> at Lohegaon. The airport’s IATA code is PNQ. The airport currently has only one terminal. It has facilities like a medical room, childcare room, restaurants, post office, retiring rooms, baggage wrapping services, snack bars, spa services, radio cab service, music store, prepaid and general taxi services, and others. Another International airport is being built near Pune in Purandar. This airport will take around five or six years to be completed and operations are expected to begin in the year 2021.</p><p>From Pune Airport, airlines such as IndiGo Airlines, GoAir, Air Berlin, Vistara, Jet Airways, SpiceJet, Air India, Air Asia, KLM, Delta Airways, and more fly to the cities of Bengaluru, Abu Dhabi, Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai, Nagpur, Goa, Kochi, Ahmedabad, Chennai, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Indore, Dubai, and others. The first flight from the city leaves at 12:20 am for Kolkata. The airline operating this flight is IndiGo Airlines. The last flight from the city leaves at 11:45 am for <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/indigo-pune-bangalore-flights.html" target="_blank">Bengaluru</a>. The airline operating this flight is IndiGo Airlines. The top domestic airline to fly from here is <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/jet-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Jet Airways</a> and the top International airline to fly from here is the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/klm-royal-dutch-airlines.html" target="_blank">KLM – Royal Dutch Airlines</a>.</p>
