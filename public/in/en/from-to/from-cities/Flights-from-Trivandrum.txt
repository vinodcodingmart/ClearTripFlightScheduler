<p>Thiruvananthapuram was formerly known as Trivandrum. It is the capital of the state of Kerala. The major industries here are sugar milling, mineral processing, handicrafts, textiles, rice and coconut cultivation, and others. It’s also home to the University of Kerala. <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/india/thiruvananthapuram/" target="_blank">Thiruvananthapuram</a> is blest with beautiful sceneries and palaces like the Kanakunna Palace and the PutheMaliga Palace Museum. Other museums that dot the city’s landscape include the Napier Museum, Natural History Museum, Museum of History and Heritage, Shri Chitra Art Gallery, and a whole lot more!Being such an important city, it is well connected to other places via its rail, roads, harbor, and airports. </p><p>Thiruvananthapuram is connected to the world via the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/trivandrum-airport.html" target="_blank">Trivandrum International Airport</a>. The airport’s IATA code is TRV. It offers facilities such as a cyber café, first aid, childcare room, foreign exchange, tourist information car rental services, prepaid taxi services, and others. The airport has two terminals for easy arrivals and departures.</p><p>Thiruvananthapuram has flights to the cities of Kochi, Kuwait, Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Bengaluru, Muscat, Mumbai, Chennai, Male, Hyderabad, Kozhikode, and more. The airlines that fly from here are SpiceJet, IndiGo Airlines, Air India Express, Maldavian Airlines, Air Arabia, Gulf Air, Jet Airways, Kuwait Airways, Qatar Airways, Etihad Airways, and more. The first flight of the day leaves the Trivandrum International Airport at 12:45 am. This is an IndiGo Airlines flight to <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/indigo-trivandrum-cochin-flights.html" target="_blank">Kochi</a>. The last flights of the day leave the airport at 10:00 pm. These are flights to Sharjah and Bengaluru. Both are operated by IndiGo Airlines.</p><p>The top domestic airline to fly from here is <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/jet-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Jet Airways</a>, and the top international airline to fly from here is <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/etihad-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Etihad Airways</a>. These airlines have won and have been nominated for a number of awards by the World Travel Awards.</p>
