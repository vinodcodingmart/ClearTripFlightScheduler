<p>Mumbai, previously known as Bombay, is referred to as the “City that Never Sleeps.” It is a true metropolitan with so much to its credit. It is the home of the glamorous Bollywood film industry. It is also the financial capital of India and has the National Stock Exchange (NSE) as well as the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE). It boasts of some beautiful and grand colonial architecture from its colonial times. It is also steeped in spirituality and has many mosques, churches, and temples. This city has a pulse of its own that is resonated within all Mumbaikars.</p><p>The <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/mumbai-airport.html" target="_blank">Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport</a> is Mumbai’s air connection to the world. The airport has a number of facilities for your convenience like foreign exchange, banks and ATMS, lounges, play areas for kids, medical centre, and even prayer and meditation rooms. For those travelling with children, the airport has childcare rooms and baby strollers too.</p><p>The city of <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/india/mumbai/" target="_blank">Mumbai</a> is connected to New Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, and more. Internationally, it is connected to major cities like Dubai, Riyadh, Sharjah, Kuwait, and Abu Dhabi among many more.</p><p>The airlines that frequently fly from the Mumbai airport are Indigo, Etihad Airways, Turkish Airlines, Singapore Airlines, Air Asia, SpiceJet, Jet Airways, and Air India. Of all these, <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/jet-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Jet Airways</a> is the leading domestic airline. This airline has won the award for India’s Best Airline from TripAdvisor in their Traveller’s Choice Awards. <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/etihad-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Etihad Airways</a> is the leading airline internationally. It has won the award for World’s Leading Airline in 2016 for the eight year in a row. This recognition was bestowed on the airline by World Travel Awards. </p><p>The first flights that departs from Mumbai airport is at 12.20 am to <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/indigo-mumbai-chennai-flights.html" target="_blank">Chennai</a>. This flight is operated by Indigo. The last flight to depart here is operated by Air India, and it flies out at 11.50 pm to <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/air-india-mumbai-singapore-flights.html" target="_blank">Singapore</a>.</p>
