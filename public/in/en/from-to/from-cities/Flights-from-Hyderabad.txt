<p>Hyderabad is a city located in South India. It is currently the capital of Andhra Pradesh as well as the new state of Telangana. It is among the very few cities in the country to be the capital of two states. This city has become a hub for commerce and trade. The industries here include informational technology (IT), textiles, and pharmaceuticals, among others. <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/india/hyderabad/" target="_blank">Hyderabad</a> is also associated with Tollywood, the Telugu language film industry. The city is well connected by rail and air.</p><p>Hyderabad is connected to the world via the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/hyderabad-airport.html" target="_blank">Rajiv Gandhi International Airport</a>. The airport’s IATA code is HYD. This airport offers facilities like help desks, WiFi connectivity, hotels, medical care, porter services, buggy services, baggage wrap services, a prayer room, and more.</p><p>The cities you may fly to from here are Singapore, Mumbai, Chennai, Bengaluru, Abu Dhabi, Delhi, Kuala Lumpur, Doha, Pune, Kolkata, Lucknow, Bhopal, London, Tirupati, Rajahmundry, Kochi, Vishakhapatnam, and others. The airlines that fly between the Rajiv Gandhi International Airport and these cities are IndiGo Airlines, TruJet, Air Asia, SpiceJet, Emirates, Oman Air, Jet Airways, flynas, Air Berlin, Etihad Airways, GoAir, Kenya Airways, JetLite, Alliance Air, Virgin Atlantic, Air Seychelles, Turkish Airlines, Delta, Air India, and others. The first flight leaves the Hyderabad airport at 12:10 am. It is a <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-schedule/flights-to-mumbai.html" target="_blank">flight to Mumbai</a> by Blue Dart Aviation. The last flight leaves the Hyderabad airport at 11:55 pm. It is a <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-schedule/flights-to-kuala-lumpur.html" target="_blank">flight to Kuala Lumpur</a> by Air Asia.</p><p>Top domestic airline here is <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/jet-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Jet Airways</a> while the top international airline is <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/etihad-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Etihad Airways</a>. Both airlines have won awards as well as been nominated by the prestigious World Travel Awards. Etihad won the “World’s Leading Airline” award while Jet Airways bagged “India’s Leading Airlines” award. The World Travel Awards was started in order to acknowledge the best in the travel, tourism, as well as the hospitality industries.</p>
