<p>New Delhi is a union territory under the Central Government located in Northern India. It is also the national capital of the country. It was given this title when in December 1911,when King George V of Britain shifted the British capital from Kolkata to Delhi. After Independence, it stayed the capital. The city is characterized by various government buildings, museums, and research institutes. Some of these are the Supreme Court Building, the Rashtrapati Bhavan, and the Parliament house. Since the city has been a center of power long before the British made it their capital, you will find architecture and other remains of the dynasties that have gone by.</p><p><a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/india/new-delhi/" target="_blank">New Delhi</a> is connected to cities all over the world via the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/new-delhi-airport.html" target="_blank">Indira Gandhi International Airport</a>.
