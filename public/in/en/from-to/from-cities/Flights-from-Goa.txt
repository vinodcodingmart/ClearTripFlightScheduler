<p>Think of beaches, and you think of Goa. The beautiful golden beaches of Goa like Baga, Calangute, Colva, Palolem, Arambol, and Morjim are the main attraction. Goa also has some fantastic wildlife sanctuaries like the Bondla sanctuary and Mollem sanctuary.</p><p>This may be the smallest state in India, but it has a lot to offer in terms of entertainment. Blessed with a natural bounty, it has the Sahyadri Mountains in the East and the Arabian Sea in the West. Goa has a unique topography, rising from sea level to a height of 1022 m.</p><p>Goa is connected to the rest of the world via the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/goa-airport.html" target="_blank">Goa International Airport</a> at Dabolim. Its IATA code is GOI. The Dabolim Airport has facilities like banks, snack bars, postal services, childcare room, medical room, spa, duty-free shopping facilities, international money exchange, ATMs, prepaid taxi services, hotel reservation counters, and a whole lot more.</p><p>The city of Goa is majorly connected to New Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai,Kolkata, and more. Internationally, it is connected to important cities like Dubai, Muscat, Doha, and Sharjah among many more.</p><p>The airlines that frequently fly from the Goa airport are Indigo, Oman Air, Air Asia India, SpiceJet, Jet Airways, TruJet, and Air India. Of all these, <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/jet-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Jet Airways</a> is the leading domestic airline. This airline has won the award for “India’s Best Airline” from TripAdvisor in their Traveller’s Choice Awards. <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/qatar-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Qatar Airways</a> is the leading airline in the international category. It has won the award for “Airline of the Year” in 2017. This recognition was bestowed on the airline by Skytrax World Airline Awards.</p><p>The first flights that departs from Goa airport is at 12.40 am to <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/indigo-goa-new-delhi-flights.html" target="_blank">New Delhi</a>. This flight is operated by Indigo. The last flight to depart here is operated by Go Air, and it flies out at 11.50 pm to <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/goair-goa-mumbai-flights.html" target="_blank">Mumbai</a>.</p>
