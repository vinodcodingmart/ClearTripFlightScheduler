<p>Bengaluru, previous known as <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/india/bangalore/" target="_blank">Bangalore</a>, is one of the top most developed cities in India and its largest. It is the capital of Karnataka. This progressive cosmopolitan is also blessed with a great climate and a vibrant social scene. It has many beautiful parks, charming cafes, and some stunning architecture from the Victorian era. Scenic spots include Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Cubbon Park, Chamaraja Lake Reservoir, and Hesaraghatta Lake. This city is growing rapidly because it is the centre of India’s Information Technology (IT) industry.</p><p>The <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/bangalore-airport.html" target="_blank">Kempegowda International Airport</a> connects Bengaluru to the world. The airport has a number of facilities for your convenience; this includes buggy service for the elderly and women with children. There’s also wheelchair access for those with reduced mobility. The airport also has free Wi-Fi, a day hotel, lounges, as well as a meet-and-assist service.</p><p>The city of Bengaluru is well connected to New Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad, Kolkata, and more. Internationally it is connected to major cities like Dubai, Colombo, and Abu Dhabi, among others.</p><p>The first flights that departs from Bengaluru airport is at 12.10 am to <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/indigo-bangalore-pune-flights.html" target="_blank">Pune</a>. This flight is operated by Indigo. The last flight to depart here is operated by Air Asia India and it flies at 11.50 pm to New Delhi.</p><p>The airlines that frequently fly from the Bengaluru airport are Indigo, Etihad Airways, Srilankan Airlines, Air Asia, Spice Jet, Jet Airways, and Air India. From these, <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/jet-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Jet Airways</a> is the leading domestic airline and has won the award for India’s Best Airline from TripAdvisor in their Traveller’s Choice Awards. <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/etihad-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Etihad Airways</a> is the leading airline in the international section. It has won the award for World’s Leading Airline in 2016 for the eight year in a row. This recognition was awarded to them by World Travel Awards.</p>
