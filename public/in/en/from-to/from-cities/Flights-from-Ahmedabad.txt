<p>Ahmedabad, also spelled as Amdavad, is one of the major cities in the state of Gujarat. <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/india/ahmedabad/" target="_blank">Ahmedabad</a> has a wealth of architecture that ranges from beautiful old mosques to behemoth contemporary structures. It is home to some fascinating museums like the Calico Museum, the Shreyas Folk Museum, and the Utensils Museums. The Sabarmati Ashram, which is the religious retreat of Mahatma Gandhi—the father of the nation—is also located here.</p><p>The city of Ahmedabad is connected to other major cities via its world-class airport, which is known as the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/ahmedabad-airport.html" target="_blank">Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport</a>. The airport’s IATA code is AMD. It is well connected to the city with buses, taxis, and car rentals that are available on a regular basis. The airport also has facilities like banks, mobile phone recharging points, shops, retail outlets, luggage trolleys, spa facilities, and a childcare room, among others. It also has special facilities like wheelchairs for traveler who may need them.</p><p>The city of Ahmedabad is connected to New Delhi, Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Lucknow, Jaipur, Kolkata, and other important cities. Internationally, it is connected to major cities like Dubai, Singapore, Kuwait, Doha, Sharjah, and Abu Dhabi, among more.</p><p>The airlines that frequently fly from the Ahmedabad airport are Indigo, SpiceJet, Etihad, Air Arabia, Jet Airways, Vistara, Goair, and Air India. Of these, <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/jet-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Jet Airways</a> is the leading domestic airline. The carrier has won the award for “India’s Best Airline” from TripAdvisor in their Traveller’s Choice Awards. <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/etihad-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Etihad Airways</a> is the leading airline in the international section. It has won the award for “World’s Leading Airline” in 2017 for the eight year in a row. This recognition was awarded to them by World Travel Awards.</p><p>The first flights that departs from Ahmedabad airport is at 2.40 am to <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/kuwait-airways-ahmedabad-kuwait-flights.html" target="_blank">Kuwait</a>. This flight is operated by Kuwait Airways. The last flight to depart here is operated by GoAir, and it flies at 11.50 pm to <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/goair-ahmedabad-mumbai-flights.html" target="_blank">Mumbai</a>.</p>
