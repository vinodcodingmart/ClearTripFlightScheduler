<h3>FAQs</h3><b>1.Can I cancel my reservation at Air Asia?</b><p>
Yes, at Air Asia you can cancel your reservations at some cost.</p><b>2.
What is the cancellation fee by Air Asia?</b><p>
The cancellation fee depends on the destination and the departure location you are traveling from. You can find the cancelation fee for your flight <a href ="https://www.airasia.com/in/en/our-fares/fees-and-charges.page">here</a></p><b>3.
Will I get a refund if I cancel my bookings?</b><p>
Yes, Air Asia gives you refunds on cancellation of your bookings, but there will be a certain cancellation fee charged according to your sector. </p><b>4.
Can I make changes in my bookings?</b><p>
Yes, you can make changes in your reservations at Air Asia through the ‘Manage Booking’ option.</p><b>5.
How can I make changes in my bookings?</b><p>
The ‘Manage Booking’ option on the Air Asia website allows you to make changes in your bookings. You can make changes in the flight timings, flight dates, your contact details, and guest details. You can also pre-book or increase the check-in baggage allowance. With the help of ‘Manage Bookings’, you can pre-book or change in-flight meals, Pick-A-Seat, or even change the seat selection along with upgrading class (only available for some flight numbers).</p><b>6.
Does Air Asia allow passengers to change the name on a reservation?</b><p>
No, Air Asia does not permit passengers to change the name on a booking.</p><b>7.
If an immediate member of my family passes away or is ill and I can’t travel, then will Air Asia refund my money?</b><p>
Yes, a refund will be made. You will have to submit a copy of the death certificate/medical certificate along with a document showing proof of relationship (E.g. marriage certificate, birth certificate, etc.)</p><b>8.
Who does Air Asia consider as immediate family members?</b><p>
Air Asia considers the following as immediate family members:</p>
<ul>
<li>Children</li>
<li>Spouse</li>
<li>Brothers and sisters</li>
<li>Parents</li>
<li>Grandparents</li></ul>
