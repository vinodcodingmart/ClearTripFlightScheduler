<h3>FAqs</h3><b>1.What is the policy for expectant mothers at Etihad Airways?</b><p>
Expectant mothers with multiple or single child can travel up to 28 weeks of pregnancy. They do not need to give a medical certificate.</p><b>2.
Till when can expectant mothers with single pregnancy travel by Etihad Airways?</b><p>
Expectant mothers can travel from the start of the 29th week until the completion of the 36th week only after submitting a medical certificate. They are not permitted to travel post that period.</p><b>3.
What is the policy for expectant mothers with multiple pregnancies?</b><p>
Expectant mothers with multiple pregnancies can travel from the start of the 29th week until the completion of the 32nd week. This traveling will require a medical certificate. They are not permitted to travel post that period.</p><b>4.
What is the policy for unaccompanied minors at Etihad Airways?</b><p>
Children between the ages of 5 and 12 can travel alone with the Unaccompanied Minor service and under the adult fare. You will be required to fill a form for the unaccompanied minors.</p><p>
Children below the age of 5 years are not allowed to travel alone and children above the age of 12 and below the age of 18 are allowed to travel alone but adult fare needs to be paid for them.</p><b>5.
Does Etihad Airways provide wheelchairs to guest with special needs?</b><p>
Yes, Etihad does provide wheelchairs free of charge to all the passengers who need assistance.</p>

<b>6.Does Etihad accept wheelchairs as baggage?</b><p>
Yes, Etihad Airways accepts wheelchair as free baggage but there are some conditions that need to be met.</p><ul>
<li>The wheelchair should be not more than 60kg</li>
<li>The height of the wheelchair should not be more than 47 inches</li>
<li>The batteries of the wheelchairs should be removed</li>
</ul><b>7.
What is the policy for infants at Etihad Airways?</b><p>
Guests below the age of 2 are regarded as infants. Infants do not require their own seat unless a child fare is purchased. Tickets for infants can be booked online while booking for the adult.</p><b>8.
I am traveling with 2 infants, what should I do?</b><p>
While traveling with 2 infants, you need to book a seat for the second infant under child fare and use an approved car seat.</p>
