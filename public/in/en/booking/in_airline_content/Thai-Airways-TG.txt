<p>Thai Airways is the largest airline of Thailand. It is committed to providing its customers the highest levels of service.</p>

<h2>On-Board Thai Airways</h2>

<p><a href="http://www.cleartrip.ae/airlines/thai-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Thai Airways</a> International Public Company Limited is the national carrier of the Kingdom of Thailand. The airline was formed as a joint venture between Thai Airways Company (TAC) and Scandinavian Airlines System (SAS).</p>

<p>It was in the year 1960 that flights were inaugurated from Bangkok to 9 overseas destinations, all within Asia. The launch of intercontinental services took place in 1971 to Australia, followed by flights to Europe in 1972, and to North America in 1980.</p>

<p>The airline has received several awards, the most notable ones being the following:</p>
<ul class="listing">
<li>Best Intercontinental Airline in the Norwegian Grand Travel Award 2012. The airline won this award for the eighth consecutive year.</li>
<li>TTG Travel Awards 2012 for Best South-East Asian Airline and Best Airline Business Class at the 23rd  Annual TTG Travel Awards 2012 Ceremony and Gala Dinner</li>
<li>Most Outstanding Airlines Full Service International Award 2011 from Travel and Hospitality (TH), an Indian print publication</li></ul>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>With its base in Bangkok, the airline operates regional and intercontinental flights within Thailand and other key destinations across the world. The Thai Airlines’ fleet consists of a variety of Airbus and Boeing aircraft.</p>

<h2>Services</h2>

<p>With the new THAI interactive AVOD system, the aircraft has larger screens and touch-control commands, offering passengers a great choice to select from the latest hit movies, evergreen classics and popular TV programs. Passengers may even learn the basics of a new language and practice meditation with this system.</p>

<p>In terms of in-flight cuisine, Royal First Class passengers departing from Bangkok enjoy an exclusive ‘Chef on Call’ service. You may request special menus in advance on most long-haul flights. Each dish is prepared by a THAI award-winning master chef.</p>
