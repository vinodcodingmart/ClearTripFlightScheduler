<p>‘Etihad’ is the Arabic word for ‘union’, and Etihad Airways defines the ideal confluence between class, service and affordability.</p>

<h2>On-Board Etihad Airways</h2>

<p>Etihad Airlines was started by the Royal (Amiri) Decree in 2003. In a short span, it has gone on to become the fastest growing airline in the history of commercial aviation. This carrier wants to bring the Arabian royal feel to their services. Cultured, considerate, warm and generous are a few of the many terms used to describe its staff.</p>

<p>As a premium airline brand, this carrier has received a range of awards including ‘World’s Leading Airline’ at the World Travel Awards in 2009, 2010 and 2011.</p>

<h2>Services and Baggage Allowance</h2>

<p>This airline offers four classes, named in true royal parlance. Diamond First, Pearl Business (flat bed), Pearl Business (seat), and Coral Economy. Passenger of all classes may take advantage of the Etihad Airways <a href="http://www.etihad.com/en-in/before-you-fly/check-in-online/" target="_blank">online check-in</a> service for getting on board conveniently.</p>

<p>Diamond First is an individual suite that offers privacy, aristocratic seats and much more. Voted World’s Best Business Class by Skytrax, Pearl Business is equipped with superb flat beds, worldwide lounge access, fine dining and other features. Pearl Business (seat) has a range of facilities like recliner seating, a la carte menu and large choice of in-flight entertainment. With warm hospitality and affordable travel rates, Coral Economy becomes a preference for many budget travelers. While it has cozy seats and tasty meals, it offers a limited Etihad Airways baggage allowance.
