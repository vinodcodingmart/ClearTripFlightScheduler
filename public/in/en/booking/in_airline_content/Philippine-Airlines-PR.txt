<p>Starting out as Asia’s first airline, Philippine Airlines (PAL) is known as a credible and quality flying alternative. One of the most respected airlines across the globe; it offers excellent service and timely flights to its passengers.</p>

<h2>On-Board Philippine Airlines</h2>

<p>PAL was started with a noble mission to serve the nation, with its first flight in 1941. This was the time of world war and the airline used a Beech Model 18 aircraft.</p> 

<p>It was officially used for commercial application in 1986. With a young fleet of planes and strong connectivity, it is one of the finest airlines in the world currently.</p>
