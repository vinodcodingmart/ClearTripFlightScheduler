<p>American Airlines (AA) is the ideal way to fly across the globe, as it offers great convenience for its passengers and connect with a number of destinations worldwide.</p>

<h2>On-Board American Airlines</h2>

<p>This carrier is a subsidiary of AMR Corporation and was founded in 1934. Along with the other subsidiaries of AMR, American Eagle and American Connection, this carrier has a wide reach. It is a founding member of the One World Alliance, the biggest aviation group globally, which offers the best to its customers.</p>

<p>AA has won several accolades for its outstanding performance in the aviation sector. The World Travel Awards voted this carrier World's Leading Airline to North America in 2012. At the 2012 Freddie Awards, it earned four honors: Airline Program of the Year, Best Elite Program, Best Promotion, and Best Customer Service.</p>
