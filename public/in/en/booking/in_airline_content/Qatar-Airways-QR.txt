<p>Qatar Airways is the national carrier of Qatar, and is headquartered at Qatar Airways Tower in Doha. It offers excellent connectivity to various locations around the world.</p>
 
<h2>On-Board Qatar Airways</h2>

<p>The airline was established on 22nd November 1993 and commenced its operations on 20th January 1994. It was re-launched in the year 1997 with a new management. In October 2012, it was announced that <a href="http://www.cleartrip.ae/airlines/qatar-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Qatar Airlines</a> would join Oneworld Alliance from 2013.</p> 

<p>It has been awarded a 5-star rating by Skytrax.  In 2011, it was also voted as ‘Airline of the Year’ in the prestigious Skytrax industry audit.</p>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>The airline currently operates 109 aircraft and has more than 250 aircraft on order. These include Boeing 777 and787 as well as Airbus A380 and A350. The Boeing 787 Dreamliner gives you a superior traveling experience with its classic combination of leading technology and design.</p> 

<h2>Services and Baggage Allowance</h2>

<p>The flights have a state-of-the-art Oryx in-flight entertainment system. This is like a multiplex in the sky! Besides, it offers an exclusive collection of duty-free products, which may be purchased on-board.</p>

<p>As far as dining options are concerned, award-winning chefs create mouth-watering dishes from the finest of ingredients. Two meals are served during a six-hour flight. Qatar Airways also accommodates special dietary requirements of passengers.</p>

<p>The Qatar Airways online check-in is a great way of saving precious time. You may avail of this facility between 36 and 2 hours prior to departure. For flights to/from USA, you may check in online between 24 hours and 90 minutes prior to departure. The Qatar Airways Web check-in allows you to choose your seat, print your boarding pass and check in your luggage with ease with the fast-bag drop counters at the airport. To check-in online, click <a href="http://www.qatarairways.com/global/en/check-in-online.page" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>

<p>The Qatar Airways baggage allowance rules permit passengers to carry 2 pieces of baggage. The weight of the checked in luggage ranges from 23 to 40 kgs based on your destination and class. For further details on baggage, click <a href="http://www.qatarairways.com/ae/en/baggage.page" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
