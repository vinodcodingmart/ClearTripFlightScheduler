<p>Spirit Airlines is an American ultra low-cost carrier headquartered in Miramar, Florida. It offers a range of services for a nominal fee, only for the services passengers want to opt for. The airline is headquartered at Miramar, Florida.</p>

<h2>On-Board Spirit Airlines</h2>

<p>Spirit Airlines was established in 1964 as Clippert Trucking Company. In the year 1974, its name was changed to Ground Air Transfer Inc. In 1983, the company started operating under the name Charter One based out of Detroit and provided tour packages to various destinations like Atlantic City, Las Vegas and the Bahamas. In 1992, Charter One changed its name to Spirit Airlines.</p>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>The airline provides connectivity to various destinations across Central America, South America, Caribbean and the United States. The aviation company’s fleet strength consists of the following Airbus fleet - A319s, A320s & A321s.</p>

<h2>Services and Baggage Allowance</h2>

<p>The airline offers its members a unique service called the $9 Fare Club. Members may avail of the ultra-low fare with fares often starting below 10 $! In addition to this, members of this club get additional baggage savings too!</p>

<p>Its frequent flyer program is called FREE SPIRIT. The miles that you accumulate as a part of this program reflects in your account status, that helps you in partner programs like Spirit World Mastercard.</p>

<p>While on-board the excellent Airbus aircraft, customers may also enjoy various beverages and snacks from the menu.</p>

<p>The Spirit Airlines baggage allowance policy for passengers has been devised in a manner to give them the benefit of paying less if they carry less luggage and vice-versa. For bags bearing weight between 18-23 kgs, the per bag charge is 25$, 23-32 kgs is 50$, 32-45 kgs is 100 $. For more than 62 linear inches it is 100 $ and more than 80 linear inches it is 150$.</p>
