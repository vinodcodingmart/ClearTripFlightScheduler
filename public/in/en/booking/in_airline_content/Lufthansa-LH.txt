<h2>On-Board Lufthansa Airlines</h2>

<p>Founded in the early 1920s after the war in Germany, Lufthansa Airways has gone through its share of evolution and reached a stage where it competes with the best in the aviation sector. Lufthansa owns many subsidiaries, like Air Dolomiti, Brussels Airlines, Edelweiss Air, SunExpress, Ukraine International Airlines, Swiss International Airlines, and much more. Its partner airlines include Jet Airways, Air India, Air Malta and Luxair, and it is the founding member of the Star Alliance, world’s largest global network for aviation.</p>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>
