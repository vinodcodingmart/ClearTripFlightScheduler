<p>Being one of Asia’s leading low-cost carriers, Tiger Airways is an airline company that offers its customers great value in terms of fares, friendly service and highest standards of safety, security and reliability.</p>

<h2>On-Board Tiger Airways</h2>

<p><a href="http://www.cleartrip.ae/airlines/tiger-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Tiger Airlines</a> was established in 2003 with Tiger Singapore. Since then, it has rapidly expanded to include Tiger Airways Australia in 2007 and associate airlines, Mandala Airlines in Indonesia and SEAir in the Philippines, in 2012. The airline won several awards, including the Low Cost Airline of the Year Award at CAPA's Aviation Awards for Excellence in 2010.</p>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>The fleet consists of Airbus A320 with an average age of less than three years and two A319s, which SEAir operates independently. As on 30th September 2012, the fleet strength was as follows - Tiger Singapore – 19 A320; Tiger Australia – 11 A320; Mandala – 3 A320 & SEAir – 2 A319 and 3 A320.</p>

<h2>Services</h2>

<p>In order to keep costs in check, the airline does not provide audio/video entertainment on any of its flights.</p>

<p>It does stock a free magazine called Tiger Tales, which gives exciting news on travel and great deals. It also has an exclusive program called Stripes, which gives you a chance to access the airlines’ great deals and discounts.</p> 

<p>An offering from the airline called Tiger Bites offers a selection of items from their menu, which may be pre-ordered online, at least 5 days prior to your flight departure. This is to ensure that you get your preferred choice of meal whenever you travel. You may only pre-order Tiger Bites at the time of booking your tickets.</p>

<h2>Baggage Allowance and Web Check-In</h2>

<p>As per Tiger Airways baggage allowance policy, they encourage customers to save costs by choosing to travel with hand luggage only. However, if you wish to check in your baggage then the airline offers you a choice of selecting the Luggage Upsize option, which needs to be selected at the time of making a reservation. A corresponding fee is applicable per passenger, per one-way flight. This excludes items such as sporting equipment.</p>

<p>For more details on luggage policies and restricted items, click <a href="https://www.tigerair.com/in/en/baggage.php" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>

<p>With the Tiger Airways online check-in facility, you need not wait in long queues at the airport. This option is available from 48 hours up to 4 hours before the scheduled departure time of your flight.</p>

<p>To avail of the Tiger Airways Web check-in facility, click <a href="https://booking.tigerair.com/WebCheckIn.aspx" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
