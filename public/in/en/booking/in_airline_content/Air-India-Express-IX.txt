<h2>On-Board Air India Express</h2>
<p>It began operations on April 29, 2005 with the first flight from Thiruvananthapuram to Abu Dhabi. It is headquartered in Kochi and predominantly caters to the Middle East and Southeast Asia.</p>
<p>It is one of the premier low-cost airlines in India and is a subsidiary of Air India, one of the major airlines of the country.</p>
<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>
<p>The Air India Express fleet consists of 21 B737-800 aircraft. The aircraft are single-class economy and have a passenger carrying capacity of around 180 seats.</p>
<h2>Services and Baggage Allowance</h2>
<p>Air India Express flights provide complimentary refreshments and mineral water to all their passengers. Snacks, reading material and other entertainment options may be purchased on-board. The courteous staff is ever willing to help out passengers and ensure that they have a pleasant time. The warm hospitality of the flight attendants and the pleasant ambience will enhance your flying experience.</p>
<p>Based on the destination, the Air India Express baggage allowance policy permits a specific amount of baggage free of charge.</p>
<p>Infants are entitled to carry free baggage weighing upto 10 kgs. Each passenger is allowed to carry one small piece of hand luggage, not exceeding 7 kgs. In case, it exceeds the limit, it will have to be checked in. </p>
<p>If the total checked-in baggage exceeds the free allowance, an excess charge would have to be paid. </p>
