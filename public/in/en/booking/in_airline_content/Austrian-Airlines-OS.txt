<p>Austrian Airlines is the flag carrier of Austria and is headquartered at Vienna International Airport, Schwechat. It is a member of the prestigious Star Alliance.</p>

<h2>On-Board Austrian Airlines</h2>

<p>In 1995, when Austria regained sovereignty over its airspace with the signature of the State Treaty, what came into existence were Air Austria and Austrian Airways. Till this point, neither of the airline companies began operating its flights. 4th April 1957 was a historic day as the two companies finally merged to form <a href="http://www.cleartrip.ae/airlines/austrian-airlines.html" target="_blank">Austrian Airlines</a>.</p>

<h2>Services and Baggage Allowance</h2>

<p>The frequent flyer program is called Miles & More. It allows you to earn miles when you fly on scheduled flights booked with Austrian Airlines, its Star Alliance partners and many other Miles & More airline partners.</p>

<p>While flying Business Class you may brace yourself to be pampered! For check-in you may straight go straight to the Business Class desk where you will be handed your boarding pass, quickly and with a smile.</p>

<p>Austrian Airlines baggage allowance policy for check-in luggage states that Economy Class passengers are allowed to board 1 bag up to 23 kgs and Business Class passengers are allowed to board 2 bags up to 32 kgs.</p>

<p>For hand luggage, Economy Class passengers are allowed 1 item weighing up to 8 kgs and Business Class passengers may carry2 pieces up to 8 kgs each.</p>
