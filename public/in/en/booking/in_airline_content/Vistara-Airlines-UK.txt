<p>Air Vistara is a joint venture between Tata Sons Limited and Singapore Airlines Limited (SIA) and aims to be known as a world-class full service carrier. The Tata Group is the majority stakeholder with a 51% holding, while SIA holds the remaining 49%.</p>

<h2>On-Board Vistara Airlines</h2>
<p>The Vistara team brings together the great hospitality and customer service experience of its parent brands to create excellence in the field of air travel. The unique name of the airline is derived from the Sanskrit word “vistaar”, which means limitless expanse, and signifies that the brand aspires to push all boundaries in its quest to be the best airline in the skies. </p>
<p>The brand palette of aubergine and gold is reflected in the strikingly patterned tails of its aircraft and in the uniforms of the crew and staff as well. The airline's logo of an eight-pointed star is made of interconnecting lines to indicate the seamless experience they hope to deliver to their customers. </p>
<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>
<p>The carrier has started operations with flights between New Delhi, Ahmedabad and Mumbai and passengers could make a Vistara Airlines online booking to these destinations. It plans to extend to more sectors and cities soon, and would be updating the route map on its website regularly. </p>
<p>The first Vistara aircraft to be revealed was an A320 that touched down on October 15, 2014 at the Indira Gandhi International Airport in New Delhi. The airline would be extending its fleet in the first few years of operation, and adding the modern A320 neo aircraft to it as well. </p>

<h3>FAQs</h3><b>1.Can I makes changes or cancel my bookings at Vistara Airlines?</b><p>
Yes, you can modify your bookings or even cancel them by calling the Vistara customer service center. You can also use the Vistara Direct portal (on the airline’s website or app) to modify your bookings.</p><b>2.
What is the cancellation fee at Vistara?</b><p>
The cancellation fee is INR 3,500 for the Economy and Premium Economy classes and INR 4,000 for the Business class at Vistara.</p><b>3.
Can I make changes after two days of booking through Vistara Direct?</b><p>
No, changes can only be made within 24 hours of booking through Vistara Direct. Post this, you will only be able to make changes via the Vistara customer service center.</p>
