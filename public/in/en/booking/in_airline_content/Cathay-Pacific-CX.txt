<h2>On-Board Cathay Pacific</h2>

<p>Founded in 1946, Cathay Pacific Airlines has a motivating past, where it grew more than 20 percent even in lean times; and a bright future that may see this carrier joining the top league. It offers scheduled cargo and passenger services to ahost of destinations around the world. This airline is the major shareholder in Air Hong Kong Limited, an all-cargo carrier that offers scheduled services in the Asia region. It is also a shareholder in the Hong Kong Dragon Airlines Limited. Besides, it is a founding member of the One World Global Alliance for the aviation sector.</p>

<p><a href="http://www.cleartrip.ae/airlines/cathay-pacific-airlines.html" target="_blank">Cathay Pacific</a> has won various awards in the aviation industry. This includes the ‘Best International Business Class’ and ‘Best International Lounges’ at the Australian Business Traveller Awards 2012.</p>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>You could make a Cathay Pacific online booking to fly to 110 destinations around the world. It has routes mainly in Asia, where it flies to over 40 prime cities, most notably to over 17 locations in China.</p>

<p>Cathay’s fleet consists of more than 130 aircraft and is one of the youngest and most modern in the skies. It has aircraft like Airbus A330-300, Airbus A340-300, Boeing 777-300ER, Boeing 777-200 Boeing 747-400, amongst others.</p>

<h2>Services and Baggage Allowance</h2>

<p>Fly comfortably with this carrier in any of four classes:</p>

<p><strong><u>First</u></strong>: This class offers the finest of features and services of the airline. You get the best recliner seats that are both elegant and provide privacy and comfort. You may pick the cuisine and entertainment of your choice and avail of exclusive lounge services at various locations. This class even offers excess Cathay Pacific baggage allowance.</p>

<p><strong><u>Business</u></strong>: Equipped with the most versatile seat of the airline, this class is ideal for business travelers. From plug points for electronic devices to choosing your entertainment option, this class has it all. Complete your journey with scrumptious, warm meals and rest comfortably in this class.</p>

<p><strong><u>Premium Economy</u></strong>: This offers the best of both, affordability and services. Here, you may enjoy wide seating, tasty snacks and meals, a range of entertainment options, and much more.</p>

<p><strong><u>Economy</u></strong>: Ideal for budget traveling, this class has seats that offer comfort as well as convenient storage space. With individual televisions, you may enjoy movies and music.</p>
