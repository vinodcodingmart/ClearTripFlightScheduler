<p><a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/premier-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Premier Air</a> is a new Indian airline expected to launch in the summer of 2015, and is being promoted by Umapathy Pinaghapani along with a group of investors. The promoters are in the businesses of information technology, distribution and retail and would provide a wealth of experience to the new enterprise.</p><p>The carrier plans to enter the domestic air travel arena in India, and received their No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the DGCA in July 2014. It is expected to provide low-cost services to make air travel more accessible but promises that high quality would be a part of the customer experience as well. It would be based at the Kempegowda International Airport in Bengaluru.</p><p>To learn more about Premier Air, check out <a href="http://www.livemint.com/Companies/Da1YrYgakhwhd4i8d6MedK/Indias-new-airline-entrepreneurs.html" target="_blank">this news article</a>.</p>
