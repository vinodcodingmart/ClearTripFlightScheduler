<p>Air Canada is the flag carrier and the largest full service airline and provider of scheduled passenger services in the Canadian market. It is a founding member of Star Alliance. The aviation company has its hubs in four major Canadian cities of Toronto, Montreal, Vancouver and Calgary. </p>

<h2>On-Board Air Canada</h2> 

<p>The airline was founded in 1936. In 2012, <a href="http://www.cleartrip.ae/airlines/air-canada.html" target="_blank">Air Canada</a> Airlines together with its Air Canada Express regional partners carried close to 35 million passengers, offering direct passenger service to more than 175 destinations in five continents.</p>

<p>To its credit, the aviation company has the below mentioned awards and recognition.</p>
<ul class="listing">
<li>In 2012, it was ranked for a third consecutive year “Best International Airline in North America” in a worldwide survey of more than 18 million airline passengers conducted by Skytrax.</li>
<li>According to the 2012 Canadian Business Travel Survey conducted by Ipsos Reid, it was the preferred airline for more than 79 percent of frequent business travellers in Canada.</li></ul>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>The airline is known for its extensive connectivity to various parts of the United States, Canada, South America, Asia and Europe. Air Canada is able to deliver on its commitment to adding value for each passenger by investing in some of the best aircraft in commercial aviation. Its current fleet includes 777-300s, 777-200s, A330-300s, 190(E90)s and CRJ 705(CRA)s, to name a few.</p>

<h2>Services and Baggage Allowance</h2>

<p>The airline promises one of the best in-flight entertainment options available on-board. With a great choice of movies and a variety of TV content, you are assured of a great experience when you fly. Through its flight notification service, passengers are able to receive alerts in case of any changes that occur to their flying schedule.</p>

<p>The airline also allows you to fly with your pets on certain flights. In addition to this, it offers efficient booking management and Web check-in services. To avail of Air Canada Web check-in facility, click <a href="http://www.aircanada.com/en/travelinfo/traveller/checkin/index.html" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>

<p>The Air Canada baggage allowance policy for complimentary luggage depends on fare, destination, date of travel and frequent flyer status. If you are flying from Delhi to Vancouver, then the weight of your bag cannot exceed 23 kgs or 50 pounds, and the dimensions of your bag must be 158 cm or 62 inches. In Economy Class, you are permitted to carry 1 bag.weighing no more than 32 kgs each.</p>

<p>Your carry-on baggage must not weigh more than 10 kgs each for standard article or personal article.</p>

<p>For more information on baggage policies, click <a href="http://www.aircanada.com/en/travelinfo/airport/baggage/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>

<p>For more details, you can contact the Air Canada customer service with the details on this <a href="http://www.aircanada.com/en/customercare/customersolutions.html" target="_blank">page</a>.</p>
