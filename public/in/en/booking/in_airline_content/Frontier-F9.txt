<p>Frontier Airlines is a low fare airline with its primary international hub at Denver International Airport, USA.</p>

<h2>On-Board Frontier Airlines</h2>

<p>The airline was launched on July 5, 1994 with two Boeing 737-200s on routes between Denver and Bismarck, Fargo, Grand Forks and Minot, N.D. During this time, the total employee strength was 180. Today, Frontier Airlines is a wholly owned subsidiary of Republic Airways Holdings Inc. The latter also owns Chautauqua Airlines, Republic Airlines, and Shuttle America.</p>

<p>The aviation company has won several awards. Some of the most notable ones include the following:</p>
<ul class="listing">
<li>Rated second in passenger satisfaction in the Market Metrix Hospitality Index for the first quarter of 2009</li>
<li>In 2008, it won the Effie award for the second consecutive year</li>
<li>In December 2006, Business Traveler magazine declared Frontier as the “Best LCC” in its annual readers’ poll</li></ul>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>Frontier Airlines flights carry passengers to major destinations in the United States, Costa Rica, the Dominican Republic, Jamaica and Mexico. The current fleet consists of the following - 16 Airbus A320s; 37 Airbus A319s & 3 Airbus A318s.</p>

<h2>Services</h2>

<p>The airline operates its business with four key principles of being affordable, flexible, accommodating and comfortable.  It offers services like Fare Options which allows you to choose the level of certainty, flexibility and amenities that you desire. It also offers the Best Fare Guarantee that assures you that you won’t find a better deal elsewhere.</p>

<p>The frequent flyer program called EarlyReturns has some of the lowest reward redemptions in the airline sector. It includes a free roundtrip flight anywhere on its domestic route system at just 20,000 miles plus 5$ in taxes and fees. There are attractive vacation packages too that are on offer.</p>

<h2>Baggage Allowance and Web Check-In</h2>

<p>The Frontier Airlines baggage allowance policy states that each passenger is allowed to board one piece of carry-on luggage and one personal item. Carry on baggage’s dimensions including wheels and handles should not exceed 9.5" height * 15.5" width * 24" length. The bag’s weight should not exceed 35 pounds. A personal item may be defined as a handbag, purse, pocketbook, backpack, briefcase, laptop with or without a bag.</p>
