<p>Finnair is a flag carrier and the largest airline of Finland. It is headquartered at Vantaa and its main hub is at Helsinki-Vantaa Airport. It is a member of Oneworld alliance.</p>

<h2>On-Board Finnair</h2>

<p>The parent company of the aviation company is Finnair Group. It has presence in Airline Business, Aviation Services and Travel Services (Tour Operators and Travel Agencies).</p>

<p>Finnair was founded on 1st November 1923.The first flight of the airline was flown on March 20, 1924 from Helsinki to Tallinn, Estonia.</p>

<p>The airline has received several awards, the most notable ones being the following:</p>
<ul class="listing">
<li>Best Airline in Northern Europe award at the 2012 World Airline Awards held at Farnborough Air Show</li>
<li>China Recognition Award 2011 at the North West Greater China Awards</li></ul>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>Finnair flights provide convenient connections to customers in Asia, North America and across Europe.</p>

<p>The fleet consists of a variety ofAirbus A330-300s, A340-300s, A321s, A320s, 319s Boeing B757s and Embraer 190s and 170s.</p>

<h2>Services and Baggage Allowance</h2>

<p>On intercontinental flights you are offered a wide selection of movies, TV series, music and games to keep you entertained. In-flight catering ensures delicious high quality meals, snacks and drinks. The menus and offerings vary depending on flight duration, time of day, destination and aircraft type. You may also indulge in in-flight shopping and entertain yourself.</p>

<p>You may also have access to a variety of newspapers and magazines, personal entertainment systems, etc. on Finnair India flights too.</p>

<p>You may save your precious time by availing of the Finnair Web check-in option. Online check-in opens 36 hours before departure. To avail of this facility, please click <a href="http://www.finnair.com/INT/GB/home-all-booking-options?SERVICECARD=3" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>

<p>The Finnair baggage allowance states that if your flight ticket is marked with the code “1 PC”, you may take one piece of baggage weighing a maximum of 23 kgs and if marked with the code “2 PC”, you may take two pieces of baggage, each weighing a maximum of 23 kgs. An excess-baggage fee will be charged for a piece of baggage weighing more than 23 kgs. The weight of a single piece of baggage should not exceed 32 kgs.</p>

<p>For carry-on baggage, the weight of the bag should not exceed 8 kgs. In addition to the carry-on baggage, you may take a small handbag, a small camera or binoculars, baby food, reading material, an overcoat or a necessary mobility aid (such as a cane or elbow crutches) on board.</p>
