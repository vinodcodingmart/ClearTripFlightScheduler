<h2>On-Board KLM Airlines</h2>

<p><a href="http://www.cleartrip.ae/airlines/klm-airlines.html" target="_blank">KLM Royal Dutch</a> Airlines was founded in 1919 to serve Netherlands and its colonies. It is one of the oldest operating airlines in the world, and has been a part of the Air France KLM group since 2004. This group also has wholly owned subsidiaries like KLM Cityhopper, transavia.com and Martinair. The airline is also a member of the SkyTeam global airline alliance.</p>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>The KLM airways carry over 75 million passengers per year and fly to more than 230 destinations in 113 countries across the globe. Operating with more than 586 aircraft, KLM has an efficient network with a modern fleet. This carrier’s performance in terms of fuel efficiency is one of the best in Europe.</p>
