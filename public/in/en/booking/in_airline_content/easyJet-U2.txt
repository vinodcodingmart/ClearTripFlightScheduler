<h2>On-Board EasyJet Airlines</h2>

<p><a href="http://www.cleartrip.ae/airlines/easyjet-airlines.html" target="_blank">EasyJet</a> was founded in 1995 with an aim to offer low-fare flights within Europe. Over the years, it has maintained high standards and is currently one of the best airlines to travel within Europe and other destinations it has added on its flight map over the years.</p>

<p>It has a strong staff of about 8,000 people, including 2,000 pilots and 4,500 cabin crew members. It has been flying over 50 million passengers and holds a premier position in the following areas - London Gatwick, London Luton, London Southend, London Stansted, Bristol, Edinburgh, Milan Malpensa, Basel, Geneva, Paris Orly, Paris Charles De Gaulle, Lisbon, Lyon, Nice & Rome Fiumicino.</p>
