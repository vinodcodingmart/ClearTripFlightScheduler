<p>Fly with United in three different classes: First, Business and Economy.</p> 

<p>The First Class offers all the luxuries you may imagine for air travel, from unwinding in lounges to full recliner seats for resting. <p>This is not all; you may get increased United Airlines baggage allowance, priority check-in and fast lines for boarding. <p>The airline has ergonomically designed seats that are comfortable, gourmet meals and drinks, and a range of in-flight entertainment. You may even shop on-board for snacks and entertainment options of your choice.</p>
