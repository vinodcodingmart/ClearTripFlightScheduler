<h2>On-Board Virgin Atlantic</h2>

<p>Founded over 25 years ago by the owner of Virgin Records, Richard Branson, <a href="http://www.cleartrip.ae/airlines/virgin-airlines.html" target="_blank">Virgin Atlantic</a> is one of the top airlines across the globe. It is known for safety, security and consistency, thanks to its focus on driving efficiency in both business and leisure markets.</p>

<p>Since 1986, Virgin Airlines has been winning awards in various sectors. It won the influential Business Traveler award in the Best Premium Economy Class and Best Airport Lounge categories, in 2011.</p>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>Virgin operates on a large network of routes across continents like Australia, Africa, Asia and Europe. It even flies to destinations in the Caribbean and the Middle East, as well as connects with countries like Mexico, UK, USA and Canada. It has partner airlines that connect with other locations where Virgin doesn’t fly.</p>

<p>Its fleet consists of Boeing 747 and Airbus A340 aircraft. It is expected to have bigger and better flights, including the world's largest double-decker aircraft.</p> 

<h2>Services and Baggage Allowance</h2>

<p>Virgin is known to be precise and timely and it offers three classes of seating and services: Upper, Premium Economy and Economy.</p>
 
<p>Once on the airplane, you will be pampered with gourmet meals, entertaining movies and music, warm and caring staff, spacious and comfortable seats, and much more.  You may even make duty-free purchases on board.</p>

<p>If you are a frequent flyer, you may join the Flying Club, a loyalty program from this airline. It enables you to earn miles to spend on rewards, upgrades, excess Virgin Atlantic <a href="http://www.virgin-atlantic.com/us/en/travel-information/baggage.html" target="_blank">baggage allowance</a>, lounge services and much more. You may add to your miles when you shop online with Virgin’s global network of car rental, hotel and airline partners.</p>

<p>If you do not wish to stand in long queues at the airport, Virgin Atlantic online check-in is the way to go. You may get your boarding pass with seating and meal preferences and directly reach the security counter before the flight.</p>
