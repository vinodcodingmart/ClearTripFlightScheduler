<h2>On-Board AirTran</h2>

<p>The year 1992 witnessed the launch of AirTran Airlines. It was created to fill the gap left by the ValuJet Airlines, Southern Airways and Eastern Airlines for the Atlanta routes at Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport. Now, this carrier is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Southwest Airlines.</p>

<p>AirTran Airways is known for its consistency and has won many accolades. In 2011, the airline quality rating board voted it the Top–Ranked Airline.</p>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>AirTran bookings are available for many domestic as well as international locations. This airline is known for offering connectivity to exotic destinations like Mexico, Caribbean, Aruba, Bahamas, Bermuda, Dominican Republic, Jamaica and Puerto Rico.</p>

<p>This airline has a fleet consisting of Boeing 717 aircraft that are created for safety and luxury.</p>
