<p>Delta Airlines is a major aviation company situated in the United States of America. It is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.</p> 

<h2>On-Board Delta Airlines</h2>
 
<p>It was in the 1920s that Delta began crop dusting and flying mail. In 1924, the Huff Daland Dusters crop-dusting operation, which formed the roots for Delta, was founded in Macon, Georgia. This was the first commercial agricultural flying company in the world. Since then, the company has grown into one of the world’s largest international airlines.</p>
 
<p>The airline has received several awards; the most notable ones include the following:</p>
<ul class="listing">
<li>In 2011, it was Ranked No.1 in Fortune magazine’s list of the world’s most admired airlines</li>
<li>In 2010, it was named Best Domestic Airline and Best Airline Customer Service – Domestic, by Executive Travel Magazine</li>
<li>In 2009, it received the Best Frequent Flyer Program, Best Airline Web Site and Best Airport Lounge by Business Traveler magazine</li></ul>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>The airline provides connectivity to most of the countries in the world. It has various hubs from which it operates, the largest one being Atlanta. The current fleet consists of 725 aircraft, which are either owned or leased by the airline.</p>
