<p>Korean Airlines is the flag carrier and the largest full service airline and provider of scheduled passenger services in the South Korean market. It is a member of SkyTeam Alliance. The aviation company has its hub at Incheon International Airport in South Korea.</p>

<h2>On-Board Korean Air</h2>

<p>The airline was founded in 1962 as <a href="http://www.cleartrip.ae/airlines/korean-air.html" target="_blank">Korean Airlines</a>. It replaced Korean National Airlines which was established in 1946.</p>

<p>To its credit, the aviation company has the below mentioned awards and recognition.</p>
<ul class="listing">
<li>In 2012, it received the 'Most Prefered Airline in China' award by Travel+Leisure magazine, China</li>
<li>In 2012, it received a certificate for number one customer satisfaction (NCSI) in domestic/international flights from the Korea Productivity Center</li>
<li>It was awarded  '2011 Grand Final World's Most Innovative Airline' by World Travel Awards</li></ul>

<h2>Connectivity and Fleet Information</h2>

<p>Korean Airlines India is known for its extensive connectivity to various countries across the globe. It is able to deliver on its commitment to adding value for each passenger by investing in some of the best aircraft in commercial aviation. Its current fleet includes Boeing 747-400s, 777-200ERs, 777-300ERs, 777-F Airbus A330-300s, A330-200s to name a few.</p>

<h2>Services and Baggage Allowance</h2>

<p>The airline promises one of the best in-flight entertainment options available on-board. With its Sky Program, the airline offers a great choice of movies and a variety of TV content, you are assured of a great experience when you fly.</p>

<p>In addition to this, it offers efficient booking management and Web check-in services. To avail of Korean Airlines <a href="https://www.koreanair.com/local/na/gp/eng/tp/wc/eng_tp_wc_info.jsp" target="_blank">Web check-in</a> facility, you may check-in any time from 4 hours to 24 hours before the scheduled departure time of your flight.</p>

<p>The Korean Airlines baggage allowance policy for complimentary luggage depends on your destination. For your checked-in luggage, if you are flying from/to Americas, then the weight of your bag cannot exceed 32 kgs per piece for First Class and Prestige Class. The numbers of pieces allowed are 3 and 2 respectively.</p>

<p>In Economy Class, you are permitted to carry 2 pieces weighing no more than 23 kgs. For more information on baggage policies, click <a href="http://www.koreanair.com/local/na/gd/eng/aa/bt/eng_bg_0101.jsp#2" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>

<p>For more details you can contact the customer service of the Airline <a href="http://www.koreanair.com/local/eu/gp/eng/cs/sm/cs.jsp" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
