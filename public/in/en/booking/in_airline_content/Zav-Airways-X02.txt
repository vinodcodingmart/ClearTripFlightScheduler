<p>Zav Airways aims at serving the Eastern and North-Eastern parts of India, and would later expand operations to the rest of the country. It is a Kolkata-based enterprise and is promoted and headed by Kishor Zavery. The airline hopes to revolutionize the industry by providing a cheaper method of private air travel to its customers. </p>
<p>The carrier plans to provide personal chopper flights by using Bell 412 helicopters that would be able to access all small and large airports with ease. It hopes to save waiting and travel time for passengers by this strategy, and the flexible and on-demand scheduling that it offers contributes to the passenger experience as well. </p>
