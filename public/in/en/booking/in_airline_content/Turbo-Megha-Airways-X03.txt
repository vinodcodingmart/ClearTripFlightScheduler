<p><a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/flight-booking/turbo-megha-airways-airlines.html" target="_blank">Turbo Megha Airways</a> is based in Hyderabad and is promoted by aviation engineer, Vankayalapati Umesh, and Telugu actor, Ram Charan Tej. It is backed by Turbo Aviation, which is an established ground services company in Hyderabad. It received the No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the DGCA in July 2014.</p><p>To begin with, the carrier plans to focus on the domestic air travel market in South India. The airline has identified 18 cities including Vijayawada, Tirupati, Rajamundry, Vizag, Coimbatore, Salem, Cadapa, Mangalore, Hubli and Belgaumthatare to be covered in the initial phase of its operations. </p><p>It would be leasing Embraer and ATR aircraft and is currently in the process of performing due diligence before taking to the skies.</p><p>To know more about the airline, do take a look at <a href="http://economictimes.indiatimes.com/industry/transportation/airlines-/-aviation/turbo-megha-airways-in-talks-to-lease-aircraft-from-embraer-atr/articleshow/40170257.cms?intenttarget=no" target="_blank">this article</a>.</p>
