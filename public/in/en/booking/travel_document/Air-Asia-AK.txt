<h3>FAQs</h3><b>1.What are the documents required at Air Asia for domestic traveling?</b><p>
The following documents are needed while traveling on domestic flights:</p><ul>
<li>Identification Card</li>
<li>Driving License</li>
<li>Passport</li>
<li>Birth Certificate, if traveling with an infant or child</li>
</ul><p>
Note: Soft copies of ID are not allowed for any Air Asia flight. In Malaysia, Driving License is not applicable as identity proof.</p><b>2.
What are the documents required at Air Asia for International traveling?</b><p>
The following documents are mandatory while traveling on international flights:</p>
<ul>
<li>Passport with six months validity</li>
<li>Visa (if required)</li>
<li>Return ticket or onward ticket (if required)</li>
</ul><b>3.
What are the payment methods available at Air Asia?</b><p>
The following are the types of payment available at Air Asia for Indian travelers:</p>
<ul>
<li>Credit card</li>
<li>Debit card</li>
<li>RuPay</li>
<li>Net banking</li>
<li>E-Gift Voucher</li></ul><b>4.
Can I cancel my bookings?</b><p>
Yes, you can cancel your bookings, but cancellation charges will be applied and rest of the value will be returned to you via an Air Asia credit account which you can use within the next 90 days.</p><b>5. 
What documents are required if I cannot fly due to illness/undergoing surgery or death of an immediate family member?</b>
If you are unable to travel due to medical reasons, then you will be required to provide a letter stating your inability to travel from a doctor.</p><p>
If an immediate family member is ill or has passed away, then you will have to provide a medical or death certificate (whichever applicable) and relationship proof, for example, marriage certificate or birth certificate.</p><b>6.
How can I make changes in my bookings?</b><p>
You can make changes to your bookings via the ‘Manage my Booking’ option.</p><b>7.
Does Air Asia charge any fee for making changes?</b><p>
Yes, changes attract a fee that should be paid along the amount charged for new purchases. The change fee may depend on the departure and destination locations.</p>
