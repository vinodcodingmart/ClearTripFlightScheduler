<h3>FAQs</h3><b>1.What documents do I need to carry for international flights?</b><p>
Apart from the flight tickets, passengers need to carry all the travel documents necessary. This includes a valid visa, passport, and other documentation required by specific countries. The passport should have six months validity from the date of travel. Embarkation forms should also be filled and submitted to the emigration authorities.</p><b>2.
Is there any fee for cancellation of booking?</b><p>
The cancellation fee for a domestic booking is INR 3,000 per passenger. The cancellation fee for an international booking is INR 3,500 per passenger</p><b>3.
Is there any fee for making changes in my bookings?</b><p>
For making changes in your bookings, you will have to pay a fee of INR 2250 per passenger for domestic flights and INR 2,500 for international flights.</p><b>4.

<b>5.What non-Indian (foreign) credit cards does SpiceJet accept?</b><p>
SpiceJet accepts cards like Visa, American Express, and MasterCard. Passengers are required to carry the card for physical verification.</p><b>6.
When will I be charged for using my debit/credit card for a booking?</b><p>
Customers will be charged only after the completion of the booking and the payment is processed.</p><b>7.
Can I change the name, date, or timing of flight in my reservations?</b><p>
SpiceJet does not allow change of name in a booking. You can change the time, date, or any other detail through reservations or the SpiceJet website.</p><b>8.
When can I make changes in my bookings?</b><p>
Changes and cancellations can be made at least 4 hours prior to the departure of the flight for international travelers and 2 hours prior to the departure of the flight for domestic travelers.</p>
