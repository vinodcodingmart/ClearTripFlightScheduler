<h1>Air Arabia Baggage Policy</h1><p>Traveling by air has just made the world a smaller place. You can enjoy the convenience and amenities en route even onboard. Flying with a carrier like Air Arabia offers you a premium air travel experience. However, certain aspects—like the amount of luggage you carry—determine how stress-free your journey will be. </p><p>To avoid the mistake of carrying excess luggage, follow important guidelines from Air Arabia’s baggage policy mentioned below. </p><h2>Checked luggage allowance</h2><p>Air Arabia baggage allowance guidelines offer passengers the flexibility of pre-booking luggage. So, you will only be required to pay for the luggage you want to carry. All you have to do is select the allowance while booking or making changes to the same. You can pick from 20 kgs, 30 kgs, or 40 kgs. The airline has no restriction on the number of pieces you would be carrying. Pre-booking your luggage online instead of purchasing it at the airport helps you save money. </p><p>If you plan to purchase check-in baggage at the airport, note that your allowance options will be restricted to one i.e. 20 kgs. If you exceed this limit, you will be charged for the same. As per the airline’s baggage rules, every piece must weigh 32 kgs or less, not exceeding the dimensions of 160 cms. </p><h2>Cabin luggage allowance</h2><p>Air Arabia’s cabin baggage allowance offers passengers the freedom to carry up to 10 kgs. The dimensions for the same, however, must not exceed 55 x 40 x 20 cms. If you are traveling with a baby aged below two years, note that he/she has a luggage allowance of a single standard-sized bag. </p><h2>Restricted items</h2><p>Air Arabia has imposed quantity restrictions on the gels, liquids, and pastes that can be carried in hand baggage. Certain items such as flammable liquids, radioactive material, cooking oils, corrosives, besides other things have been prohibited by the airline. </p><p>Since packing your bags involves a lot of adding and eliminating to meet the permissible limits, you need to ensure that unnecessary items are excluded from the lot. This will help you avoid any inconveniences related to excess baggage. For more details on the luggage policy of Air Arabia, click <a href="http://www.airarabia.com/en/baggage" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
