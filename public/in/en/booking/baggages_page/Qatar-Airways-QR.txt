<h1>Qatar Airways Baggage Policy</h1><p>Qatar Airways ranks amongst the world’s top airlines. If you have chosen this carrier to fly to your destination, you’ll be glad to know that it is highly acclaimed for its services. Preparations galore, it’s important that you don’t lose focus from the most important part – your luggage. </p><h2>Checked luggage allowance</h2><p>Qatar Airways’ baggage policy largely depends on the destination you are traveling to and your cabin class. If you are traveling from a specific destination to India, the checked luggage allowance for First Class passengers is 50 kgs. For Business Class passengers, the allowance is 40 kgs. Economy Class travelers are permitted 30 kgs. Passengers are expected to ensure their check-in baggage adheres to the overall dimension limit of 300 cms regardless of the cabin class. The airline charges for excess baggage. </p><h2>Cabin luggage allowance</h2><p>The permissible hand baggage weight and number of pieces will vary as per your cabin class booking. If you have a First or Business Class ticket, you can carry 2 pieces having a combined weight of 15 kgs. However, if you are flying Economy Class, you are permitted 1 piece not exceeding 7 kgs. The dimensions for each piece of hand luggage must not exceed 50 x 37 x 25 cms. Apart from the standard cabin baggage allowance for each class, every passenger is permitted to carry personal items. </p><h2>Restricted items</h2><p>The airline has made it mandatory for passengers to carry electronic devices like laptops, games, DVD players, and tablets as part of check-in luggage only. Explosives, flammable liquids/solids, corrosives, and other such items are strictly prohibited in checked luggage. Passengers are also not allowed to carry stunning devices, workers tools, blunt instruments, etc. as part of hand luggage. </p><p>Now that you have a clear idea, it’s time you knock those extra kilos off your baggage. For added details on Qatar Airways’ baggage allowance, click <a href="http://www.qatarairways.com/in/en/baggage.page" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
