<h1>Lufthansa Baggage Policy</h1><p>When you are booking your flight, it is important to ensure that the airline you are flying with offers a generous baggage allowance. If you have booked with Lufthansa, this shouldn’t be a problem. However, you still need to pay attention to the weight and number of pieces you carry just to ensure you don’t exceed the set limit by the carrier. </p><p>Here is an overview of Lufthansa’s baggage policy as a guide to help you before packing. </p><h2>Checked luggage allowance</h2><p>Lufthansa’s baggage allowance will depend on your class of travel. If you have booked an Economy Class ticket, you can carry 1 bag weighing up to 23 kgs. On a Premium Economy Class booking, you will be permitted 2 bags, with the weight of each not exceeding 23 kgs. If you opt for a Business Class fare, you can carry 2 bags, with each weighing not more than 32 kgs. On a First Class ticket, you get the freedom to carry up to three bags, with each weighing 32 kgs or less. </p><p>The same check-in baggage rules are applicable to children who are 2 years old and above. </p><h2>Cabin luggage allowance </h2><p>Lufthansa determines the hand baggage allowance for every passenger as per the cabin class booked. So, if you are traveling via Economy or Premium Economy Class, the allowance is 1 piece not exceeding 8 kgs. However, if you are a Business or First Class passenger onboard a Lufthansa flight, the cabin baggage allowance goes up to 2 pieces weighing 8 kgs each. </p><h2>Restricted items</h2><p>The airline prohibits passengers from carrying items including the Samsung Galaxy Note 7, explosives, pressurized containers, flammable liquids, and radioactive materials, besides other things. </p><p>Follow these guidelines while packing for your Lufthansa flight to avoid any inconvenience. However, if you need more details on the luggage policy of Lufthansa, click <a href="http://www.lufthansa.com/us/en/Free-baggage-rules" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
