<h1>United Airlines Baggage Policy</h1><p>You probably know by now that baggage rules are integral to a smooth flight journey with almost any airline. Knowing how much your carrier allows in terms of both checked and cabin luggage is important. However, very often the confusing luggage rules could have you heading in the wrong direction. Don’t worry and skim this article if you want to know every detail about United Airlines’ baggage policy. </p><p>Here’s an insight into the carrier’s baggage-related rules and regulations. </p><h2>Checked luggage allowance</h2><p>As per United Airlines’ baggage allowance rules, United Economy passengers are permitted 23 kgs of checked luggage. United Business, United First, United Polaris first class, and United Polaris business class passengers may carry up to 32 kgs of check-in baggage. The MileagePlus Premier status will also determine the baggage allowance. </p><p>Anything crossing the set limit for each cabin class will be charged as per excess baggage rules. </p><h2>Hand luggage allowance</h2><p>The airline allows 1 piece of hand baggage in addition to a personal item. However, this rule is not applicable to holders of a Basic Economy ticket. Passengers who have opted for this fare type are only permitted 1 personal item. </p><h2>Restricted items</h2><p>The airline does not allow passengers to bring along items such as explosives, oxidizers, compressed gases, corrosives, loaded firearms, flammable liquids, flammable solids, radioactive materials, poisons, fireworks, and much more. There are restrictions on the quantities that passengers could carry in terms of items such as hairsprays, perfumes, deodorant, besides other things. </p><p>These details should be taken into consideration when you are packing for the journey. However, if there you require additional information regarding the luggage policy of United Airlines, click <a href="https://www.united.com/web/en-US/content/travel/baggage/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
