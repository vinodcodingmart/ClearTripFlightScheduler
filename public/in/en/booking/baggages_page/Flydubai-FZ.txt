<h1>Flydubai Baggage Policy</h1><p>Ensuring that all the nitty-gritties of your journey are taken care of before flying is very important. If you’re traveling via flydubai, it is essential that you are informed of the carrier’s regulations with regard to the weight, number of pieces, and banned/restricted items. </p><p>To get detailed information on flydubai’s baggage policy, read further. </p><h2>Checked luggage allowance</h2><p>As per flydubai’s baggage allowance regulations, Economy Class passengers need to pre-pay for check-in baggage. They may choose to buy 20, 30, or 40 kgs, depending on the availability of space on the aircraft. Purchasing 30kgs/40kgs of luggage is subject to sufficient space on the aircraft. You can carry up to 3 pieces, provided that their combined weight does not exceed the allowance you have paid for. </p><p>Business Class passengers are allowed to check-in 3 pieces of luggage having a total weight of 40 kgs or less. Both Economy and Business Class passengers must ensure that the weight of each luggage piece does not exceed 32 kgs. </p><h2>Cabin luggage allowance</h2><p>The cabin baggage allowance for Economy Class passengers is 1 piece. The weight of this item should not exceed 7 kgs. Apart from this, they can also carry additional items like a handbag/satchel, duty-free shopping bag, or laptop bag. However, the total weight of both items must not cross 10 kgs. </p><p>Business Class travelers may carry 2 pieces of hand luggage that have a combined weight of 15 kgs. </p><h2>Restricted items</h2><p>flydubai does not allow certain items in checked and cabin luggage. These include lithium batteries, illegal products, and hoverboards, besides other things. Certain things like toy weapons, sharp objects, hypodermic needles, and sporting bats/clubs are also not allowed in hand baggage. </p><p>Now pack easily by following the above rules and restrictions applied by the airline. For further information on the luggage policy of flydubai, click <a href="https://www.flydubai.com/en/flying-with-us/baggage/" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
