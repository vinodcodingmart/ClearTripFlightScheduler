<p>A city in Uttar Pradesh, Agra will bewitch you with its spectacular culture and architecture. Best-known for its splendid Mughal era monument, notably the Taj Mahal, this city located on the banks of the River Yamuna is a treat for architecture and history lovers.</p><p>The city is not only home to one of the Seven Wonders of the World; it is also home to a number of other significant monuments like the Agra Fort, Jama Masjid, and the Tomb of Akbar.</p><p>The old city charm of Agra can be witnessed as you walk through the lively local markets. The narrow lanes, colorful handicrafts, silver jewelry, and pretty accessories, welcome you as soon as you step in the market. The city also has a lot to offer to its visitors in terms of delicious food and sweets, like jalebi and petha. Don’t forget to savor the Dal Moth when in Agra.</p><p>The city of Agra is easily accessible to tourists, thanks to the local <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/agra-airport.html" target="_blank">Agra Airport</a>. The Agra Civil Enclave also referred to as the Kheria Airforce Station is a public airport and military airbase, located 5 kms from the city of Agra. The airport has only one terminal, for both arrivals and departures.</p><p>Traveling to and from the airport is quite easy as you can make use of car rental services that are available at the airport.</p><p>If you are flying to Agra between the months of July and September, be careful as there are heavy rains which often disrupt flight timings and also cause severe traffic jams in the city.</p>
