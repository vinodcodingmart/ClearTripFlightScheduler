<p>If there’s one place in India that is known for its natural beauty and its awe-inspiring mountains, it is without any doubt the city of Leh. The high-altitude desert city, in the Himalayas, is the capital of the Leh region in northern India’s Jammu and Kashmir state. The city draws a large number of domestic and international tourists. With its pleasant climate, the city is open and welcoming to tourists in both the summer and winter months.</p><p>Its flawless natural beauty, steep rocky ridges, and snow-covered mountain peaks create the perfect setup for a range of activities like trekking, mountain biking, rafting, and mountaineering. Explorers and adventurers can be sure of out-of-this-world experiences and will fall in love with the city.</p><p>The moment you land in Leh, the clean air and clear blue skies above will take your breath away. The snow-clad peaks, mountain flowers, deep valleys, and magnificent streams that flow through it will give you the tranquility that most seek but few find.</p><p>You can reach Leh easily by flight, as it is connected via the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/leh-airport.html" target="_blank">Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport</a>, also known as the Leh Airport, which is located at 10,000 feet above sea level, making it one of the highest airports in the world. The total capacity of its single terminal is 150 passengers for departures and arrivals.</p><p>The bus stand is located just 5 kilometers from the airport. Prepaid taxis and other buses are also easily available within the airport area, making it convenient for passengers to reach their destinations. At a distance of 5.5 kilometers from the airport, the railway station is also conveniently located, improving connectivity and comfort.</p>
