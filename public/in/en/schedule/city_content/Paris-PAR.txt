<p>Paris is the capital city of France, and one of the most loved cities in Europe. The city radiates beauty and warmth and you can’t stop yourself from falling in love with it. The graceful architecture of monuments like Notre Dame stand in contrast to the modern architecture of structures like Centre Pompiduo, both appealing in their own way. If you are looking for a romantic escape, head to the River Seine.</p><p>Paris is among the topmost tourist destinations in the world, flaunting its culture, art, history, music, architecture, and romance. It is the world’s most popular destination for honeymooners and couples. The city is a treat for those who appreciate art as there are a number of art galleries that serve as the art repositories of the world.</p><p>As one of the most important tourist destinations in the world, Paris is well connected, with two international airports in the city - Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) and Orly Airport (ORY).</p><p>The <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/paris-airport.html" target="_blank">Charles De Gaulle Airport (CDG)</a> is the primary international airport of Paris. It is located in the Roissy region around 30 Km to the northeast of Paris. There are eight terminals at Charles de Gaulle International Airport – Terminals 1, 2A, 2C, 2D, 2E, 2F, 2G, and 3. Frequent shuttle services run between the terminals for the convenience of passengers. You can reach this airport via buses, taxis, and rental cars.</p><p>The Orly Airport (ORY) is the second international airport of Paris and is near to Disneyland Paris and at a distance of 19 Km from the city center. The airport offers domestic and international flight services through its two terminals – Terminal South and Terminal West. The Orly Airport can be accessed by buses, taxis, and the automated metro system.</p><p>Before you visit Paris, it would be a good idea to learn a few words of French, as not everyone can communicate in English.</p>
