<p>San Francisco is a city with a resilient and outgoing culture that will leave tourists head over heels in love with its beauty and energy. Yes, with the ‘Silicon Valley’, the city may be the headquarters for some of the world’s biggest corporates, but there’s a lot more that it has to offer. San Francisco is all about beautiful beaches, cable cars, sunny days in the parks, streets lined with sophisticated Victorian homes, and spectacular cityscapes. There are innumerable museums and gardens to visit here that will delight you with their beauty, history, and culture.</p><p>To witness the grandeur of the city head to the Golden Gate Bridge that offers an enthralling view of the city. If you want to know the truth about the fascinating stories about the Alcatraz Island, head there for an eerie, but fascinating experience. San Francisco is a melting pot of cultures and the city lives up to its reputation, with something to offer for everyone.</p><p>You can easily reach this visually appealing city, as the <a href="https://www.cleartrip.com/tourism/airports/san-francisco-airport.html" target="_blank">San Francisco International Airport (SFO)</a> is located in the Bay Area in the city and is close to the city center of San Francisco. It is the largest airport in the city. San Francisco International Airport has four terminals – Terminals 1, 2, 3, and the International Terminal. Terminals 1, 2 and 3 cater exclusively to domestic arrivals and departures. All of these terminals are connected by the AirTrain service.</p><p>The airport can be reached via Caltrain and BART services that connect the airport to nearby areas. You can even opt for rented cars, vans, shared vans, airport buses, taxis, and charter buses to go to and from the airport.</p>
