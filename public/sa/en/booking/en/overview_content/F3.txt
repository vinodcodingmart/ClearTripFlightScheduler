</p>
<p>
    Saudi Arabia’s flag carrier, Saudia announced the commencement of
    affordable airline Flydeal on 17 April 2016. The venture aims to make it a
    world-class airline by the year 2020. It has been established to cater to
    domestic travellers, pilgrims, and tourists. The flights started operating
    on 23 September 2017, after a year of its establishment, linking two major
    cities of Saudi Arabia, Jeddah and Riyadh.
</p>
<p>

    establishment in 2016, it has been growing its network rapidly. Last year,
    on November 30, 2017, it has achieved a major milestone by flying its
    100,000th Customer. With headquarters in Jeddah, the King Abd EL Aziz
    International airport is its major hub. The airline has recently joined the
    ICAO, International Civil Aviation Organization.
